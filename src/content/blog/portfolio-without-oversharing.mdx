A public investing page can be useful without exposing every private detail.

The goal here is to make the portfolio legible: asset allocation, region allocation, currency exposure, and percentage return. The raw values stay private. That boundary keeps the page focused on process and risk rather than account size.

Manual entry is a feature for now. It makes every update deliberate, easy to audit, and independent of broker integrations that would require a much larger security surface.

Nothing on the page is investment advice. It is a record of one person's thinking and a prompt to review assumptions over time.
